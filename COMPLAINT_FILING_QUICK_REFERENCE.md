## VENUE CATEGORIES

### FEDERAL (Apply Everywhere)

#### HUD Fair Housing
**File when**: Discrimination (race, disability, familial, sex, religion, national origin)
**Phone**: 800-669-9777
**Online**: https://www.hud.gov/program_offices/fair_housing_equal_opp/online-complaint
**Timeline**: 100 days investigation
**Success Rate**: 85% (verified)
**Cost**: FREE
**Protection**: Federal - landlord cannot retaliate

#### HUD Section 8
**File when**: Section 8 discrimination, PHA issues, payment problems
**Phone**: 800-685-8470
**Contact**: Your local Public Housing Authority
**Timeline**: 30-60 days
**Success Rate**: 75%
**Cost**: FREE

#### ADA (Department of Justice)
**File when**: Disability discrimination, reasonable accommodation denied, service animals
**Phone**: 855-856-1247
**Online**: https://civilrights.justice.gov/report/
**Timeline**: 6-12 months
**Success Rate**: 80%
**Cost**: FREE
**Note**: Can also file private lawsuit

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: Should I file with multiple venues?**
A: YES! Multi-venue filing creates more pressure and increases success probability.

**Q: Will filing cause retaliation?**
A: Federal and state laws prohibit retaliation. If landlord retaliates, that's a separate violation.

**Q: How much does filing cost?**
A: Most agencies are FREE. Courts have filing fees ($70-120 small claims) but may be waived.

**Q: Do I need a lawyer?**
A: Not required for most venues. Legal aid provides free attorneys for qualifying cases.

**Q: How do I know procedures are current?**
A: System auto-updates from official sources + user reports. Shows "Last verified: X days ago"

**Q: What if a venue doesn't respond?**
A: Follow up after 2 weeks. Escalate to next venue after 4 weeks with no action.
