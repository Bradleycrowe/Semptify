SORTING RATIONALE
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

HOME:
- First touchpoint → client management belongs here
- "Who am I helping?" before "What do I need?"

LIBRARY:

- Reference material separate from doing tasks

CALENDAR (★ Key Innovation):
- Combined timeline + evidence because time context is everything
- Documents without dates = useless in court
- Evidence without timeline = unorganized chaos
- Rent payments = calendar events with proof
- Natural question: "What happened when?" → Upload proof there

TOOLS:
- Power features for advanced users
- AI and utilities separate from core workflow
- System maintenance hidden from basic users

HELP:
- Safety net category
- External resources and services
- Crisis support beyond legal issues
