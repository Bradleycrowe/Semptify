Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 20, 2025

1. Information We Collect

Semptify is a tenant rights protection platform. We collect:

  • Anonymous User Tokens: 12-digit numeric tokens for authentication (no email required)
  • Document Storage: Files you upload to your vault (stored in your chosen cloud provider)
  • OAuth Tokens: Temporary access tokens for Google Drive or Dropbox (stored encrypted)
  • Usage Data: Logs for security and troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Authenticate you with anonymous tokens (no personal identifiers)
  • Store your documents securely in your own Google Drive or Dropbox
  • Generate court-ready complaint packets and timeline evidence
  • Provide tenant rights guidance and legal form generation

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your documents live in YOUR cloud storage (Google Drive or Dropbox), not our servers
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted using AES-256 with PBKDF2HMAC key derivation
  • Token hashes (SHA-256) are stored locally for authentication
  • We never see your plaintext password or token

4. Third-Party Services

When you connect cloud storage, you authorize:

  • Google Drive API: To create a .semptify folder and store encrypted data
  • Dropbox API: To create a .semptify folder and store encrypted data

These services have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Privacy Policy
  • Dropbox Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

  • User tokens: Retained until you delete your account
  • Documents: Remain in YOUR cloud storage (you control retention)
  • OAuth tokens: Expire per provider policy (Google: ~1 hour, Dropbox: long-lived)

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (via your cloud provider)
  • Delete your data (delete files from your Drive/Dropbox)
  • Revoke OAuth access (via Google/Dropbox account settings)
  • Request account deletion (contact support)

7. No Selling of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Period.

8. Children's Privacy

Semptify is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. Changes will be posted at https://semptify.onrender.com/privacy.

10. Contact Us

Questions? Email: privacy@semptify.com (or use GitHub issues)
