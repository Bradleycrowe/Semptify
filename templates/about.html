About Semptify

Empowering tenants with legal knowledge and self-advocacy tools

Our Mission

Semptify provides Minnesota tenants with the tools, resources, and guidance needed to understand their rights, document housing issues, and prepare legal documents for self-representation in housing court.

What We Do

  • Legal Library - Access Minnesota housing statutes, tenant rights guides, and court procedures
  • Document Vault - Securely store evidence with cryptographic attestation and OCR for handwriting
  • Complaint Filing - Generate court-ready packets with guided wizards
  • Calendar & Timeline - Track deadlines, rent payments, and case events
  • Research Tools - Look up landlord history, property violations, and court records
  • AI Assistance - Get answers to legal questions (educational purposes only)

Educational Purpose

Important: Semptify is for educational and self-help purposes only. It does not provide legal advice and is not a substitute for an attorney. For legal representation, please consult a licensed attorney.

Open Source & Privacy

Semptify is built with privacy-first principles:

  • Anonymous 12-digit token authentication (no email required)
  • User-controlled cloud storage (Google Drive or Dropbox)
  • AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive data
  • Local-first architecture with optional cloud backups
  • No tracking, no ads, no data selling

Technology Stack

Built with: Flask, Python, SQLite, ReportLab (PDF generation), EasyOCR (handwriting recognition), and AI integration (OpenAI, Azure, Ollama)
