üõ°Ô∏è Your Tenant Rights

Knowledge is Power - Understand Your Protections

üè† Right to Habitable Housing

  • Clean, safe, and sanitary living conditions
  • Working plumbing, heating, and electricity
  • Structural integrity and weather protection
  • No health or safety hazards

How Semptify Helps: Document violations with timestamped photos/videos, track repair requests, and maintain evidence of conditions.

üîê Right to Privacy

  • Landlord must give proper notice (usually 24-48 hours)
  • Reasonable times for entry
  • Emergency exceptions apply
  • No harassment or repeated unnecessary entries

How Semptify Helps: Log all landlord contacts, entry attempts, and maintain a timeline of privacy violations.

üíµ Right to Fair Treatment

  • No discrimination based on protected classes
  • Reasonable rent increases (check local laws)
  • Return of security deposit with itemization
  • Proper eviction procedures must be followed

How Semptify Helps: Track rent payments, deposit receipts, and document discriminatory practices with evidence.

üìú Right to Proper Notice

  • Written notice for lease changes
  • Proper eviction notice periods
  • Service of process requirements
  • Right to contest improper notices

How Semptify Helps: Store all notices securely, track service dates, and document any improper service attempts.

‚öñÔ∏è Right to Due Process

  • Right to defend in eviction proceedings
  • Right to present evidence
  • Right to legal representation
  • Right to appeal

How Semptify Helps: Organize all evidence chronologically, generate court packets, and maintain complete documentation.

üõ†Ô∏è Right to Repairs

  • Timely response to repair requests
  • Emergency repairs within 24 hours
  • May have "repair and deduct" rights
  • Cannot be retaliated against for requesting repairs

How Semptify Helps: Document repair requests, track response times, and maintain photo/video evidence of issues.
