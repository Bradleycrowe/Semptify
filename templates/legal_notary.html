Legal Notary

This page helps you record a legal notarization completed with a licensed notary (in person or remote). It does not perform a notarization.

Where to get a legal notarization?
  • Banks/credit unions often provide notary services to members.
  • Shipping stores (UPS Store, FedEx Office) and some libraries offer notaries.
  • Remote Online Notarization (RON) providers are available in many states (e.g., Notarize, OneNotary). Check your state's rules.
