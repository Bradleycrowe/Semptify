# Module Inventory

## Working Modules

1. **attorney_trail**
   - **Status**: Integrated and functional.
   - **Purpose**: Provides a step-by-step guide for attorneys.
   - **Route**: `/law_notes/attorney_trail`

2. **complaint_templates**
   - **Status**: Integrated and functional.
   - **Purpose**: Generates complaint templates based on jurisdiction and type.
   - **Route**: `/law_notes/complaint_template`

3. **mn_jurisdiction_checklist**
   - **Status**: Functional.
   - **Purpose**: Provides a checklist for Minnesota jurisdiction.
   - **Route**: `/mn_checklist`

4. **law_notes_actions**
   - **Status**: Functional.
   - **Purpose**: Provides various actions related to legal notes, such as generating demand letters, uploading evidence, and exporting packets.
   - **Routes**:
     - `/check_broker`
     - `/file_broker_complaint`
     - `/generate_demand_letter`
     - `/identify_owner`
     - `/file_owner_complaint`
     - `/attach_evidence_packet`
     - `/upload_evidence`
     - `/group_evidence`
     - `/export_evidence_packet`
     - `/export_multilingual`
