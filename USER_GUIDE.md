# Semptify User Guide - Complaint Filing

## Overview

Semptify automatically analyzes your documents and fills out legal complaints for you. Upload your lease and eviction notice, and the system does the rest.

## Quick Start (5 Minutes)

1. Register account
2. Upload documents (lease, notices, emails)
3. View case assessment
4. Generate complaint (auto-filled)

## Step-by-Step Workflow

### 1. Upload Documents

Go to /vault and upload:
- Lease agreement (required)
- Eviction notice (required)
- Rent receipts (recommended)
- Communication with landlord (recommended)
- Photos of property issues (optional)

System automatically:
- Extracts landlord/tenant info
- Identifies rent amounts and dates
- Analyzes legal clauses
- Calculates case strength

### 2. View Case Assessment

Dashboard shows:
- Case Strength: 0-100% (based on documents)
- Win Probability: Tenant vs Landlord advantage
- Timeline: Chronological events
- Next Steps: What to do next

### 3. Review Perspective Analysis

Click any document to see 4-angle analysis:
- Tenant View: Your rights and risks
- Landlord View: Their position
- Legal View: Clause validity
- Judge View: Likely ruling

Scores range -100 to +100. Positive = advantage.

### 4. File Complaint (Auto-Filled)

Go to /file-complaint

System pre-fills:
- Your name, address, phone (from lease)
- Landlord name, company, phone (from lease)
- Property address (from lease)
- Rent amount (from lease)
- Key dates (move-in, first issue, notice received)
- Issue description (from timeline)

Confidence: 60% typical (review and complete missing fields)

### 5. Select Evidence

System ranks documents by relevance:
- 95%: Lease agreement
- 90%: Eviction notice
- 85%: Rent receipts
- 80%: Communication emails

Top 3-5 usually sufficient for complaint.

### 6. Generate Court Packet

Click \"Generate Packet\" to create PDF with:
- Completed complaint form
- Ranked evidence list
- Timeline summary
- Case strength assessment
- Perspective analysis

Ready to file in court.

## Tips

**For Best Results:**
- Upload clear, readable documents
- Include all rent payment records
- Document all communication with landlord
- Take photos of property issues with dates

**Case Strength Factors:**
- Lease violations by landlord: +30%
- Complete payment history: +20%
- Written maintenance requests: +15%
- Property code violations: +20%
- Timeline documentation: +15%

**Common Issues:**
- Missing signatures: Reduces legal score
- Incomplete payment records: Lowers case strength
- No written communication: Harder to prove claims
- Unclear lease terms: Difficult to enforce

## FAQ

Q: How accurate is the auto-fill?
A: 60% average. Always review and complete missing fields.

Q: Can I edit auto-filled data?
A: Yes, all fields are editable before generating packet.

Q: What if case strength is low?
A: System suggests missing documents to improve strength.

Q: How long to generate packet?
A: Instant once documents uploaded and analyzed.

Q: Do I need a lawyer?
A: System helps with paperwork. Consult attorney for legal advice.
